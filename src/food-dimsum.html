Dimsum

Servings : 4-6 servings

Cook Time : 30 minutes

Steps

  1. Take a food processor and grind the prawns add the ground pork and the rest of the ingredients, to mix everything well
  2. Take the wonton skins and place between your thumb and the forefinger, add the filing in the middle and carefully push up the rest of the skin and push with a teaspoon the filling. Flat the base so the dim sum can sit straight on the steamer.
  3. Take a pan filled it with water and place your bamboo steamer on the top and basket with a leaf of Chinese cabbage o baking paper with holes so you can put on top your dim sums.
  4. Form into balls: Shape mixture with greased hands into evenly sides meatballs, about 1 1/4-inches each (or about (38g).
  5. Put the dim sum in the bamboo steamer basket, cover and steam for about 12 min
  6. Served with soy sauce.
